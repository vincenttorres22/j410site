"We Are All Born Naked, and the Rest is Drag"

-RuPaul Charles

Cal Poly's Drag Club is dedicated to delivering mesmerizing art and entertainment through gender performance.

The club has been an outlet for queer individuals and allies since 2015 to express themselves in ways that they felt they previously couldn't..

Paint Nights

Drag Club Paint Nights

Every other week, Drag Club hosts paint nights in order for members of the club and aspiring drag performers to get help with anything they need before a performance. This can range anywhere from wig styling to makeup tips and tricks. Usually off campus, these paint nights are a great way for members to socialize and come together outside of school.
